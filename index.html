about me

My name is Ana and I'm a front-end web developer that loves bringing people's ideas to life by building accessible, clean and responsive websites.

Prior to transitioning into tech, I worked as R&D engineer in polyurethane industry in China, and as office manager in consulting industry in Toronto. I love scuba diving so that passion drove me to become PADI scuba instructor as well.

When not coding, you will find me trying out new plant-based recipes, scuba diving, traveling, hiking, watching dachshund and/or corgis funny videos or just chilling.
